About

About Me

I am currently pursuing a Master of Laws in Ethnology at Minzu University of China in Beijing. I received my Bachelor of Laws in Sociology from Minzu University of China, with minors in Anthropology and Archaeology.

My research combines comparative historical methods, ethnography, oral history, computational text analysis, machine learning, and bibliometric analysis. I am especially interested in ideology, memory and forgetting, the social history of culture, and the relationship between structure, agency, and political-cultural transformation.
